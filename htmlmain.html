Butterfly Family

The butterfly families include:

1. Pieridae, the whites and sulfurs, known for their mass migrations;
2.Papilionidae, the swallowtails and parnassians;
3. Lycaenidae, including the blues, coppers, hairstreaks, and gossamer-winged butterflies;
4. Riodinidae, the metalmarks, found chiefly in the American tropics;
5.Nymphalidae, the brush-footed butterflies; Hesperiidae, the skippers;
6. Hedylidae, the American moth-butterflies (sometimes considered a sister group to Papilionoidea).

The brush-footed butterflies represent the largest and most diverse family and include such popular butterflies as the admirals, fritillaries, monarchs, zebras, and painted ladies.
